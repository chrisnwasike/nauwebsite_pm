About the University

A Beacon of Knowledge
in West Africa

Nnamdi Azikiwe University is a leading Federal Government university located in Awka, Anambra State. Founded in 1992 and named after Nigeria's first President, UNIZIK is dedicated to producing globally competitive graduates who drive innovation, leadership, and social transformation.

🏛️
Academic Excellence
Nationally accredited programmes taught by distinguished faculty with international research credentials.
🔬
Research & Innovation
State-of-the-art laboratories and research centres producing solutions for Nigeria's most pressing challenges.
🌍
Community Impact
Deeply embedded in the Anambra community — serving over 30 years as an engine of social and economic development.
