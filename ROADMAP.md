# Public Roadmap – UNIZIK Institutional Website & CMS Platform

**Target Completion:** May 6, 2026  
**Total Duration:** 4 weeks (April 8 – May 6, 2026)

---

## Week 1 – April 8–14, 2026

- Infrastructure provisioning & environment setup
- Multi‑tenant routing engine (tenant detection, URL rewriting)
- S3‑compatible object storage connection (media subdomain foundation)
- Core database schema & migration

---

## Week 2 – April 15–21, 2026

- MediaService implementation (upload pipeline, image variants, WebP conversion)
- Main CMS authentication & role‑based access control
- University homepage (dynamic, connected to CMS)
- News & events engine (main domain)

---

## Week 3 – April 22–28, 2026

- Faculty subdomain framework (shared templates, tenant‑scoped data models)
- Faculty CMS – basic content management (news, events, staff)
- Department & programme data structure
- Cross‑domain news aggregation (main site feed from faculties)

---

## Week 4 – April 29 – May 6, 2026

- Full faculty rollout (all 6 subdomains live)
- Media library & asset management (all tenants)
- Performance optimization (caching, image delivery, Lighthouse ≥80)
- Security hardening & final QA
- Production deployment & handover

**Status as of April 8:** All milestones on track for May 6 delivery.
